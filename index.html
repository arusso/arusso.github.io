Bobbie (a mediawiki bot framework)

Named after a lost friend, Bobbie is my sidekick at work. Bobbie helps me automate the maintenance of my system documentation by helping me quickly grab data from any source I can obtain programatically, and input it with relative ease into MediaWiki.

Currently, I use it to create new host pages in our wiki whenever a new host is provisioned, as well as updating tags for host on a wiki page with facts from Puppet/Facter.
